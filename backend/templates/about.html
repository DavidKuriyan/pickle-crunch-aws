Our Story

Three generations of pickle-making wisdom, now available at your fingertips — powered by the cloud.

From Grandmother's Kitchen to Your Doorstep

Pickle&Crunch began in 1995 in a small kitchen in Andhra Pradesh, where our grandmother would wake up at dawn to hand-grind spices, sun-dry vegetables, and lovingly prepare batches of pickles that the whole village would come to buy. Today, we honour that same tradition while embracing modern technology to bring these flavours to you — wherever you are.

Every jar is made in small batches, with the same recipes passed down through our family. No artificial preservatives. No shortcuts. Just pure, honest, homemade goodness stored in AWS DynamoDB for real-time freshness tracking.

Why Cloud + Pickles?

We believe great food deserves great technology. By running on AWS EC2 and DynamoDB, we can track stock in real time, accept orders 24/7, and scale during festival seasons — so you never miss out on your favourite Gongura Mutton or Dry Fruit Laddus.
